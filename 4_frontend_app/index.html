⚕️ Offline First Aid Guide

CPR Basics

Check responsiveness. Call SOS. Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 beats per minute).

Checking Splints / Fractures

Do not try to realign the bone. Immobilize the injured area using a makeshift splint (wood/magazines).

Frostbite & Hypothermia

Move to a warm room. Remove wet clothing. Warm the core first, not limbs. Give warm beverages.
